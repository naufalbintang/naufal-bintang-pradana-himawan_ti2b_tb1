About Myself

I am a passionate and determined student pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Informatics Engineering (D4) at Politeknik Negeri Jakarta, driven by my long-standing fascination with programming and problem-solving. My journey into tech began in junior high school, where I first discovered the thrill of creating solutions through code. This interest led me to specialize in Computer and Network Engineering (TKJ) during vocational high school (SMK), where I graduated in 2023.


After not passing the UTBK exam that year, I made the deliberate decision to take a gap year to refine my skills and reattempt the test. That year of focused self-study not only strengthened my academic foundation but also reaffirmed my commitment to a career in technology. In 2024, my perseverance paid off when I secured admission to Politeknik Negeri Jakarta's Informatics Engineering program—a pivotal step toward my goal of becoming a skilled software developer.


I thrive on challenges that blend logic and creativity, whether it's debugging code, designing efficient systems, or collaborating on projects. Beyond academics, I actively explore emerging technologies and contribute to open-source communities to expand my practical knowledge. When I'm not coding, you'll find me tinkering with hardware, reading about tech trends, or mentoring peers who share my enthusiasm for programming.
